Here's a sample content for a sick leave application:

**Subject: Application for Sick Leave**

Dear [Supervisor's Name],

I am writing to inform you that, due to health reasons, I will be unable to attend work from [start date] to [end date]. I am currently under medical treatment and my doctor has advised me to take a break from work to recover.

I am providing a medical certificate from my doctor, which is attached to this email, to support my leave application. The certificate confirms that I am unfit to work due to [briefly mention the illness or medical condition].

I apologize for any inconvenience this may cause and will make sure to catch up on any missed work as soon as I am able to return to the office. If there are any pressing tasks that need my attention in the meantime, please let me know and I will do my best to complete them remotely.

I would like to request [number] days of sick leave, as recommended by my doctor. I will ensure that my work is up to date and completed as soon as possible upon my return.

Please let me know if there are any additional requirements or documentation needed to process my leave application. I appreciate your understanding and support during this time.

Thank you for your attention to this matter.

Sincerely,
[Your Name]

**Attachments:**

* Medical certificate from your doctor
* Any other supporting documents, if required

**Note:**

* Make sure to customize the content to fit your specific needs and company policies.
* Keep the tone professional and polite.
* Attach any required documentation, such as a medical certificate, to support your leave application.
* Proofread your application carefully before submitting it to your supervisor.
