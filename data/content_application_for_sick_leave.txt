Here's a sample content for a sick leave application:

**Subject: Application for Sick Leave**

Dear [Supervisor's Name],

I am writing to inform you that I will be unable to come to work due to [briefly mention the reason for your sickness, e.g. illness, injury, or medical procedure]. I am currently under [doctor's/hospital's] care and am expected to be out of the office for [number of days] days.

I apologize for any inconvenience this may cause and will make sure to catch up on any missed work as soon as I am able to return to the office. I will provide a doctor's note or medical certificate to support my absence, if required.

My expected date of return to work is [date]. If there are any pressing matters that need my attention in the meantime, please let me know and I will do my best to address them remotely.

Thank you for your understanding and support during this time. If you have any questions or concerns, please do not hesitate to reach out to me.

Sincerely,
[Your Name]

**Additional Information to Include:**

* Date of application
* Employee ID number (if applicable)
* Job title and department
* Contact information (email and phone number)
* Any relevant medical documentation (e.g. doctor's note, medical certificate)

**Tips:**

* Be honest and clear about the reason for your sick leave
* Provide as much notice as possible
* Offer to provide a doctor's note or medical certificate, if required
* Keep your supervisor informed of your expected date of return to work
* Be available to answer any questions or concerns your supervisor may have
